Contact Support

In-App Support Navigation

To reach Help & Support in the app:

  1. Open the Dinamik Management app
  2. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  3. Select Help & Support

You'll find sections for FAQs, Tips & Tricks, support contact, privacy policy, feedback options, and app information (including version and update checks).

Need Immediate Assistance?

While we don't offer phone support at this time, the fastest way to reach us is via email at dinamikmanagement@iriedinamik.org. We monitor the inbox daily and aim to respond promptly.
