Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Overview

Dinamik Management ("the App") helps landlords and property managers organize property information, tenant records, finances, and maintenance workflows. We designed the App to keep your data under your control. This privacy policy explains what information the App accesses, how that information is stored, and the limited circumstances in which it might be shared.

Data Ownership and Control

  • You own your data. All property, tenant, financial, maintenance, and document information you enter into the App belongs to you.
  • Local-first storage. By default, all data stays on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect or upload your information to our servers.
  • Optional sync. If you enable iCloud sync in the App's settings, your information is stored in your personal iCloud account, protected by Apple's encryption and security controls.
  • No third-party analytics. The App does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or external data brokers.

Information the App Processes

Category Examples Storage
Property information Addresses, purchase details, utilities Local / iCloud
Tenant & lease details Names, contact info, lease terms Local / iCloud
Financial records Rent payments, expenses Local / iCloud
Maintenance & issues Tasks, complaints, future work Local / iCloud

Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Face ID / Touch ID / Optic ID: Used only to gate access to sensitive data when you enable "Require Authentication for Sensitive Data."
  • Contacts, Phone, and Messages: Only used when you initiate a call, SMS, or email from within the App. We do not access or store your contact list.
  • Calendars: Optional integration to add showings and maintenance events to your personal calendar.
  • Photos: Used when you attach photos to rooms, items, maintenance tasks, or documents.

Security Controls

  • Biometric protection: Enable authentication in Privacy Settings to require Face ID/Touch ID before viewing sensitive details.
  • Masked data: Turn on masking for financial and tenant contact information.
  • Auto-lock options: Configure how quickly the App locks when inactive.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The only times data may leave your device are:

  1. iCloud Sync: When you opt in, Apple securely syncs your App data across your devices.
  2. Exports or sharing initiated by you: When you email a report or share a document.
  3. System backups: Your data may be included in encrypted device backups managed by Apple.

Children's Privacy

Dinamik Management is intended for property owners and managers. It is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Choices

  • Manage sync: Enable/disable iCloud sync in Settings.
  • Control authentication: Configure biometric requirements in Privacy Settings.
  • Delete data: Remove individual records or delete the App to remove local data.
