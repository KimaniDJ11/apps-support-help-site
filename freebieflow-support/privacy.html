FreebieFlow — Privacy Policy

FreebieFlow (“the App”) helps you discover and manage offers. We designed the App to keep your data under your control. This privacy policy explains what information the App accesses, how that information is stored, and the limited circumstances in which it might be shared.

Data Ownership and Control

  • You own your data. All entries, preferences, and saved offers belong to you.
  • Local-first storage. By default, all data stays on your device. We do not collect or upload your information to our servers.
  • No third-party analytics. The App does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or external data brokers.

Device Permissions

  • Notifications: Optional, used to alert you about offer reminders you enable.
  • Photos: Only if you choose to attach images; selected files remain on your device.

Security Controls

  • Local protection: Data stays on-device and follows your device’s security (passcode/biometrics).
  • Masked data: Sensitive details remain hidden unless you choose to reveal or share them.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The only times data may leave your device are:

  1. Exports or sharing initiated by you: When you share offers, screenshots, or feedback, the data you include is sent to your chosen destination.
  2. System backups: Your data may be included in encrypted device backups managed by your platform provider.

Children’s Privacy

FreebieFlow is intended for adults. It is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Choices

  • Manage permissions: Use your device settings to change notification or photo access.
  • Delete data: You can remove individual entries in the App. To delete all data, remove the App from your device.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as we enhance the App. Material changes will be communicated via release notes or in-app messaging. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
