FreebieFlow: App Support

This is the official support page for FreebieFlow, a web app that tracks services offering free credits or trials. It reminds you when renewals are due and keeps your "used" vs "active" services organized.

Contact

Email: freebieflow@iriedinamik.org

FAQ

Q: What is FreebieFlow?
A: FreebieFlow is a web app that tracks services offering free credits or trials. It reminds you when renewals are due and keeps your "used" vs "active" services organized.

Q: How do I add a new service?
A: Click the "Add Service" button, fill in the service name, associated email, number of free credits, and the renewal date, then hit Save.

Q: Can I edit or delete a service later?
A: Yes. Click on a service to edit it, or use the delete button to remove entries you no longer need.

Q: What does the "Used" toggle do?
A: Marking a service as "Used" moves it to the Used section so you can focus on remaining freebies. When the renewal date passes, FreebieFlow automatically resets it to Active with the next renewal date.

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Your data never leaves your device and is completely private.

Q: How can I request a feature or report a bug?
A: Open a GitHub issue or email us at freebieflow@iriedinamik.org.
