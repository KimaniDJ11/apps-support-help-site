Connect & Play Together.

How do I connect for Multiplayer?
  1. Connect to Wi-Fi: Ensure both you and your partner are on the same Wi-Fi network (or use Bluetooth).
  2. Host Game: One person taps "Host Game" to create a lobby.
  3. Join Game: The other person taps "Join Game" and selects the Host's device.
  4. Accept: The Host accepts the connection request.

Trouble connecting? Check that "Local Network" permissions are enabled in iOS Settings.

How do I win?
  • Classic Line: Mark 5 squares in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  • Blackout: Mark ALL 25 squares on the board.
  • Four Corners: Mark the 4 corner squares.
What does "Retiring" an activity do?
When you finish a game, you can "Retire" completed activities. This hides them from future games so you always get fresh date ideas. It does not delete them permanently, just filters them out of the match pool.
App not loading?
Try force quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, check for updates in the App Store.
Missing cards or purchase?
Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to make the purchase. You can also try the "Restore Purchases" button in the settings menu.
Notifications disabled?
Re-enable notifications by going to your iOS Settings → Notifications → CouplesBingo.
