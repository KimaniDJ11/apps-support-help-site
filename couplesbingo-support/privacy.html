Data Minimization

CouplesBingo is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal data needed for gameplay and synchronization.

When you play with a partner, game state data is transmitted securely to facilitate the multiplayer experience. We do not inspect or store this gameplay data on our servers for any purpose other than facilitating the real-time game.

We do not sell your personal information. Any account information used (such as Apple ID for cloud sync) is managed by Apple's services, and we do not have direct access to your credentials.
