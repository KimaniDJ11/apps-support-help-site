FAQ

  • App crashes: Force quit and reopen. If it persists, reinstall the app.
  • Purchases missing: Ensure you’re on the same Apple ID; tap “Restore Purchases” in-app.
  • Notifications: Enable in iOS Settings → Notifications → CouplesBingo.
